# Bark & Stroll: canned replies for Facebook leads

## Short reply (best for comments on the hiring post)

> Hey, thanks for the interest! Here's our short application with all the details: https://barkstroll.com/join.html

## Slightly warmer DM reply (Michele-style first contacts)

> Hi! Thanks so much for reaching out, really appreciate it. Here's our short application with all the details: https://barkstroll.com/join.html. It's about 5 minutes. Once it's in I'll review it personally and reach back out. Talk soon!

## Reply to "what's the minimum age?" comments

> Walkers need to be 18 or older. Full details and application here: https://barkstroll.com/join.html

## Reply after they apply (use after a strong application lands)

> Hey [name], thanks for applying. Read through it and it sounds great. Want to grab a quick call this week? Free [day] [time]?

## "Not a fit" rejection (after Scott marks them rejected in portal)
